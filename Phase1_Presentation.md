## Slide 2: Project Overview & Problem Statement
*   **Problem:** Traditional credentialing systems are prone to fraud, difficult to verify, and lack AI-driven insights.
*   **Solution (BlockCredAI):** A decentralized, AI-powered credentialing platform that ensures authenticity, tamper-proof records using Smart Contracts, and intelligent verification using AI services.
*   **Goal of Phase 1:** Establish the core architecture, set up the frontend and backend environments, and initialize smart contract development.
